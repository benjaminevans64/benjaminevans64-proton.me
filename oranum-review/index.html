Our Full Review

Oranum Overview

Oranum takes a unique approach to online psychic readings with its live video streaming model. Instead of booking blind, you can watch advisors in free live streams and decide if their style resonates before spending any money.

The Streaming Experience

Oranum’s differentiator is the free live video format. Advisors broadcast regularly, and anyone can join to watch and participate in public chat. This “try before you buy” model is unlike anything else in the psychic industry.

Going Private

When you find an advisor you connect with, you can request a private reading using credits. The transition from public stream to private session is seamless, and you already know the advisor’s style.

Our Verdict

Oranum’s streaming model is innovative and transparent. The ability to preview advisors risk-free is a major advantage. While quality varies more than on curated platforms, the preview feature means you’re unlikely to be disappointed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Oranum legit and safe to use?

Yes. Oranum has operated since 2011 and uses TLS for all payments. Users can sign up with a pseudonymous nickname. The main risk users report is runaway spend during private video — set a credit cap in account settings before starting.

How much does a reading cost?

Private readings start at 1.99 credits/min (~$1.98) for newer advisors and scale to 9.99+ for headliners. Free streams are genuinely free; you only spend credits for tips or private rooms.

Do the 9.99 free credits have strings?

They expire within 72 hours of activation and can only be spent on private-session minutes. No card required to claim.

Can I refund credits?

Oranum’s terms state credits are non-refundable once used. Support will credit back technical faults on their side; they will not refund a reading you simply didn’t like.

Is the free live stream really free?

Yes — you can watch and chat for as long as you like without any cost. Tipping or going private is the monetisation.
